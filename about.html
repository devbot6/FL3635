About Us

Established in Warner Robins, Georgia, our Houston County First Robotics Team has been participating in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) since 2011. As an esteemed organization, FRC challenges teams to design, build, and program robots to complete specific tasks. Our team has earned notable recognition, competing in numerous FRC events, including the World Championships. Our team is composed of highly talented and motivated students from esteemed institutions such as Houston County High School, Veterans High School, Northside High School, Perry High School, and Warner Robins High School. Each season, we work diligently within strict timelines and objectives, facing real-world challenges that promote STEM education and inspire young people to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Through our participation in FRC, we strive to foster excellence in all aspects of STEM education, demonstrating our commitment to innovation and problem-solving. As we tackle each challenge, we hone our skills and build upon our collective knowledge, preparing ourselves for the dynamic and ever-changing demands of the future.
